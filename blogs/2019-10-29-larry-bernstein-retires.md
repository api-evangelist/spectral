Larry Bernstein, one of the original five scientists (along with Fritz Bien, Mike Gersh, Dave Robertson, and Jim Elgin), who founded SSI in January of 1981, has retired. He was the last of the 5 still working at SSI! Larry has been a significant influence, mentor, friend and co-worker for over 38 years. Larry was celebrated Hawaiian style on October 29, 2019.
